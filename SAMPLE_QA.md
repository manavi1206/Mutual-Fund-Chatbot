## Query 8: How to Redeem

**User Question:**  
"How do I redeem my HDFC Large Cap Fund units?"

**Assistant's Answer:**  
To redeem HDFC Large Cap Fund units:

1. Log in to your Groww account
2. Go to "Mutual Funds" section
3. Select the HDFC Large Cap Fund from your portfolio
4. Click on "Redeem" or "Withdraw"
5. Enter the number of units or amount you want to redeem
6. Confirm the transaction

The redemption proceeds will be credited to your registered bank account within 3-4 business days.
