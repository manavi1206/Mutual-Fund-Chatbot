Where can I get the transaction history?
You can request your transaction history/order history in the 'Reports' section of your profile. 
Steps to request your T
ransaction/
Order History: 
Go to your profile. 
Select the 'Reports' option. 
Under 'Transactions', choose the desired report. 
Select the time frame and click 'Download'. 
Note: 
The password to open the report is your PAN number in capital letters. 
Click the button below to go directly to the 'Reports' section.
