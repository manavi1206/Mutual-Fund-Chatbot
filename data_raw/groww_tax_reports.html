Where can I get the tax proof for section 80C?

You can request your tax proof for Section 80C by downloading the Mutual Fund-ELSS statement. 

Steps to request your ELSS Statement: 
  1. Go to your profile. 
  2. Select the 'Reports' option. 
  3. Under 'Tax', find 'Mutual Fund - Capital Gains'. 
  4. Choose the financial year and click 'Download'. 
Click the button below to go directly to the 'Reports' section.
